On this page are all the rules you must follow when you are on LaughLoud.

Content Guidelines and Rules:
  1. Inappropriate Language:
    • Avoid the use of offensive language, including but not limited to explicit profanity. Mild language is acceptable, but strong swears are not allowed (e.g., avoid using offensive words).
  2. Self-Promotion:
    • Refrain from promoting personal or commercial interests within the joke content. The platform is for sharing jokes, not for advertising products, services, or personal gain.
  3. Respectful Content
    • Do not target or make jokes about specific individuals. Content should be light-hearted and enjoyable for a wide audience. Avoid offensive or hurtful remarks.
  4. Category Relevance:
    • Ensure that every joke is appropriately categorized. Each joke should align with the designated category to maintain a well-organized and enjoyable experience for users.
Moderation Policy:
  1. Content Review:
    • All submitted jokes will be subject to review to ensure they adhere to the content guidelines. Jokes violating these rules may be removed.
  2. User Reporting:
    • Users are encouraged to report any content that violates the guidelines. Reports will be reviewed promptly.
  3. Warning and Education:
    • Users found in violation of the rules will receive a warning. The moderation team may provide feedback on the specific rule violation.
  4. Repeat Offenders:
    • If a user consistently violates the guidelines, their jokes may be deleted, and they may face a temporary or permanent ban from the platform.
